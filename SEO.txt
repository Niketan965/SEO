Q1
Search engine optimization (SEO) is the process of improving a website and/or web content to increase its visibility and SERP ranking. The importance of SEO is that it: increases the chances of your website showing up in search results. Higher-ranked websites are more likely to be found by users who are actively searching for your product, service, or information. SEO improves the usability of your website and reduces bounce rates and engagement. It’s a more cost-effective approach than paid advertising. It includes mobile optimization so that you can reach users on their mobile devices You need to invest in SEO in order to stay competitive in today’s digital landscape. Search engine optimization tools provide you with valuable data for marketing decisions SEO allows you to reach international markets by improving your SEO for different regions/languages SEO adjusts to changing search engine algorithms so that you can maintain or improve your rankings SEO plays an essential role in improving your website’s visibility, credibility and user experience resulting in increased organic traffic resulting in overall business success.

Despite the fact that search engine algorithms are constantly changing, meta tags still offer several advantages in terms of web development & online marketing. On-page SEO: meta tags can still help improve your search engine rankings by increasing your click-through rate. User Engagement: A well-written meta description can make users click on your pages in search results. Identity: Meta tags strengthen your website’s identity by reflecting your brand, authorship, and copyright. Structured data: Some meta tags allow you to include rich snippets in your search results that provide additional information. Social media sharing: Meta tags control the way your content appears on social media. Mobile-friendliness: meta tags ensure that your content is mobile-friendly and responsive, making your content more appealing to users. Content Security Policy: Some meta tags, such as Content Security Policy, help protect your website from cyberattacks by increasing your website's security. Robot meta tags: Meta tags are used by robots to control how search engines index and follow links. Meta tags allow you to collect data on user behavior as well as tracking scripts.

Favicon is short for “favorite icon.” It’s a small, usually square, image or icon that’s associated with a particular website. Favicon images can be found in many places on your web browser, but they’re most commonly found next to your website’s title or page title on browser tabs, in bookmarks and in the address bar of your browser.
